Why Not - Friends.

I like math rock. I really do. I feel like it’s something that really takes me over when it’s done well. And this is done well.

A few dudes from Minneapolis create a really mature sound. It's full of raw power balanced off gentler melodies. There's a lot of variation between tracks which leaves you with a lot to explore on the album. Off the hop with DEME/\/TIA you'll find yourself immersed in this this erratic groove and once your in, hang on because the ride's just getting started.

They're a young band and with as much time to continue to grow and refine their sound as these fellas have, I'm really excited to hear what they'll come up with next.

Start With Dear Friend Part 2, IULM, and Fall Apart.
